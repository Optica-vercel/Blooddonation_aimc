Eligibility Screening Test

Ensure you satisfy the primary criteria before checking in.

1. Age & Weight Check

Are you between 18-60 years old and do you weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs)?

2. Health Status

Are you feeling completely well today and did you sleep at least 6 hours last night?

3. Donation Interval

Has it been more than 3 months (90 days) since your last whole blood donation?
