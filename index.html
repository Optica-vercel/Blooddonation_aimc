Blood Work & Screening

Safety and health screenings for our active donors

AIMC Blood Donation Society is committed to donor safety and clinical health standards.

If you get your blood work done through our network, we will help you with complimentary screenings including Hepatitis B & C, HIV, Malaria, and Syphilis.

All blood work screenings are processed confidentially through verified diagnostic laboratories at Allama Iqbal Medical College and Jinnah Hospital Lahore.
